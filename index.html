Stefano Meschiari
W. J. McDonald Postdoctoral Fellow
University of Texas at Austin

Hello! I am a postdoctoral astronomer at the University of Texas at Austin, working to find new exoplanets and understand how they formed.

I love modelling data, and I especially enjoy creating tools that empower others to understand and extract meaning from data.

Finally, I am passionate about open-source software development. I create cool-ish things like games for astronomy education, code to discover exoplanets, and all sorts of small toys and hacks.

Hailing from Modena, Italy
Currently in Austin, Texas
Husband + dog
Research
I am interested in the discovery and characterization of extrasolar planets, the formation of planets in disturbed environments, evolution of protoplanetary disks, citizen science-driven projects and hydrodynamical and N-body codes.
